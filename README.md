This repository contains the source code for the digital platform of AcroYoga Punta Cana. Designed with a modern architecture to reflect the brand's core essence: clean movement, absolute efficiency, and real community.

The site showcases our main services and the philosophy behind our training, moving away from holistic clichés and focusing entirely on pure technique and human connection.

The content of the platform is structured around our three core teaching pillars:

𝟬𝟭 / 𝗔𝗰𝗿𝗼𝗯𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗰𝘀
Mastering gravity through technique. We elevate the body to the next level starting from the foundations. Initial flights, team dynamics, and continuous transitions (washing machines). All backed by years of experience in sports education and the science of moving with total efficiency.

𝟬𝟮 / 𝗠𝗼𝗯𝗶𝗹𝗶𝘁𝘆
A strong body without flexibility is a limited body. Structural preparation before flight. We integrate calisthenics and functional mobility to open hips, release shoulders, and decompress the spine.

𝟬𝟯 / 𝗖𝗼𝗺𝗺𝘂𝗻𝗶𝘁𝘆
Individual success is always a shared achievement. Training loses its power in isolation. We build spaces for genuine human connection through outdoor Jams and events across the Dominican Republic.

✦ Ｓｅｒｖｉｃｅｓ
⯌ Hotels & Resorts Experiences: Premium wellness for guests seeking an exclusive challenge during their stay.
⯌ Corporate Team Building: Outdoor physical challenges that demand absolute coordination, real trust, and effective communication under pressure.
⯌ Classes & Training: Technical progressions for all levels, focusing on biomechanics and bone stacking.
